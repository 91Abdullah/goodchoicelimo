AIRPORT PICK UP PROCEDURES:

Your driver will meet and greet you at the gate area with a name sign, help you with your luggage and escort you to the Vehicle. Inside Meet & Greet Service is additional $39.00 (Optional) Or Look for pre-arranged signage in baggage claim area.

Terminal 1: Go to PRE-ARRANGED LIMOUSINE area on the outside platform DOOR # A and ask the commissionaire (in blue uniform) to call Elite Towncar Vehicle from the Designated waiting area. Your car will arrive shortly After being paged. Should you encounter any problem, Call us toll free 1-866-408-0039 and one of our agents will be glad to help you.

Terminal 3: Go to PRE-ARRANGED LIMOUSINE area on the outside platform POST # 29 and ask the commissionaire (in blue uniform) to call Elite Towncar Vehicle from the Designated waiting area.
